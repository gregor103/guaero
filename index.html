About

GU Aerospace is a new society created by current Aeronautical Engineering students at the University of Glasgow. We are open to anyone who is currently studying an Aerospace Engineering or related degree. In addition to this, we encourage any students with a general interest in Aerospace engineering to get involved! We created the society to give students the opportunity to network with each other, alumni and industry. Our members can expect socials, networking events, site visits and more!
